Step 4: Starting the bot for the first time

Initializing the bot

We are almost ready, once again locate the folder where your DevBot template is and from there run this command:

npm start

Wait for all the modules to load, and that's it! Congratulations, you built your first bot! 🤖🤖🤖🤖🤖🤖🎉🎉🎉🎉🎉🎉🎉🎉😀😀😀😀😀😀 Interact with the bot, ask him things!.

So...... what now?

Well, you can start looking at the code, what he's doing when you ask him a question. Type "source" to navigate the templates and start playing with that. Your first bot is up and running, do you want to know more? Ask me about:
